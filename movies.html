Oppenheimer

Oppenheimer is a really good movie. It's the first movie I've seen in quite a bit and it's amazing. The cinematography for this movie is so good and i love the way every looks. The acting is also superb.Oppenheimer is a biopic and the movie isn't necessarily about the bomb but more so Oppenheimer and the politics surrounding Oppenheimer during and after the bomb. Another thing i like about this movie is that the "main villain" Lewis Strauss reason for wanting to tear Oppenheimer down and revoke his security clearance was because he thought that Oppenheimer talked bad about him to Einstein when in reality they talked about something completely different. This movie is a masterpiece and is one of my favorite movies I've seen I'd give it a 5/5
