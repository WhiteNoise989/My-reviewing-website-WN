Frieren: Beyond Journey's End

Frieren Beyond Journey's End is a show that i think if you haven't seen it already you absolutely should .Frieren follows the journey of an elven mage who, after defeating the Demon King with her party of heroes, begins to face the reality of her long lifespan compared to her human companions. As centuries pass and her friends age and die, Frieren embarks on a new journey to understand the meaning of friendship, mortality, and the human experience. This concept alone got me interested in the show. And when i stared to watch it i could see why its one of the highest rated anime on MAL. The animation, The story, The characters are all amazing and that's me even mentioning the sound track. The soundtrack is for this show is incredible. The animation is amazing it looks hand drawn almost and some shots like in ep 25 are works of art. Overall I'd definitely give it a 5/5 i highly recommended this show.

Kaguya sama love is war

Kaguya Sama love is war. Is a show that should be way more popular then it already is. This show (And manga) is really good and one of the better romance shows I've seen now granted I haven't seen many but still. The characters in this show are very likable and well written and the comedy in this show is also very well written and actually funny at some points. The two main characters Kaguya and Shirogane and very well written and the comedy of them over thinking and making plans are great. As for the side characters like Ishigami, Chika and Iino they are also very well written and get alot more development and focus in the manga later on. Chainsaw Man

Chainsaw Man isn't like most Shonen anime. This anime is just weird but it's also really good. The main character Denji isn't like most main characters. Most main characters have some sort of goal like wanting to be the hokage or killing every titan. Denji's main goal is to touch boobs and its great. This story is just so well written with so many weird and insane moments like in ep 8 where Denji and one of the side Characters Himeno kiss. But she ends up Throwing up inside his mouth. Stuff like that that make this show so different from any other show I've watched and i think that demonstrates just howe good of a writer Fujimoto is. And the manga right now is really good with the introduction of the war and death devil. Im excited to see where this story goes and I'm so excited for the bomb devil arc movie. This show is just really good and is in my top five anime. 5/5

Dandadan

I'll start this off by saying i haven't watched the Dandadan anime. BUT i have read the manga and what i can say is that this show is really good. Dandadann was made by Yukinobu Tatsu and he was on Fujimotos team for Chainsaw man and it shows. this show starts off in the weirdest way possible.Momo believes in ghosts but not aliens and Okarun believes in aliens but not ghosts. In a bet to determine who is correct, the two decide to separately visit locations associated with both the extraterrestrial and the occult Momo visiting the former, and Okarun the latter. Momo is abducted by a group of aliens and Okarun gets possessed by a spirit who takes over his body and takes his balls. The rest of the show follows the both of them trying to get Okarun's balls back. This show is once again a show that is unlike anything I've seen and its great. The chemistry between Okarun and momo is perfect and the other side characters like Jiji,vamola,Aira are also really good. Overall this show is really good and I'd give it a 4.8/5
